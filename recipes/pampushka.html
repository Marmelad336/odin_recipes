Recipes Pampushka

Description

A simple recipe for ideal pampushki for borscht! Cook them in a Cosori air grill - it will be incredibly tasty and extremely healthy

Ingredients

  • Milk 140 ml
  • Chicken egg 1 pc
  • Salt ½ tsp
  • Sugar ½ tbsp
  • Dry yeast 1 tsp
  • Flour 280 gr
  • Butter 60 gr
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Dill sprigs, finely chopped 8 pcs
  • Garlic clove, finely grated 6 pcs
  • Vegetable oil 20 ml

Steps

  1. Mix salt, sugar, egg and yeast in warm milk (no more than 40°C). Pour the mixture into the flour and mix well
  2. Melt the butter and add to the dough. Cover the dough with a towel and leave in a warm place for 40-60 minutes
  3. Divide the dough into parts, knead and roll into small buns (approximately 50 g each). Place on a sheet of baking paper and leave for another 15-20 minutes
  4. Place the mesh tray in the Cosori airfryer basket
  5. Select the “Preheat” program, set the temperature to 160℃, then press Start/Pause
  6. Place the paper with the buns on the preheated mesh tray. Select the “Bake” program or manually set the temperature to 160℃ and time to 20 minutes, then press Start/Pause
  7. Mix vegetable oil, garlic, dill, salt and pepper. Grease the finished buns with the mixture
  8. Serve warm with borscht
